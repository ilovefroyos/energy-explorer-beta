about the project

The Community Energy Explorer aims to build public energy literacy. By interacting with highly visual and contextually relevant information, Metro Vancouver citizens will be encouraged to uncover how energy is an intricate part of their community. This project invites the public to learn, engage and participate in the development of energy conscious communities that will sustain our region's exceptional quality of living.

Energyexplorer.ca is being developed and produced by the Collaboration for Advanced Landscape Planning in partnership with Lloma Design, and has been supported by the Neptis Foundation, the Real Estate Foundation of BC and Metro Vancouver.
